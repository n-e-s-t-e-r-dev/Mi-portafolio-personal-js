Description

Rick and Morty is an Adult Swim animated science fiction comedy series that follows Rick Sanchez, an extremely intelligent and alcoholic scientist, and his grandson Morty Smith, who often have incredible adventures in alternate realities

The television series Rick and Morty was created by Justin Roiland and Dan Harmon. The concept was inspired by Roiland's animated short interdimensional Cable 2 that was produced for Channel 101. The show was co-developed by Adult Swim and Justin Roiland and Dan Harmon are the executive producers of the show, alongside Mike McMahan.
